What is this club about?

The VIT Chennai Mathematics Club is a lively community of students who explore the beauty and power of mathematics beyond the classroom. Through workshops, guest lectures, contests, and research circles we transform abstract theory into real-world insight and spark a lifelong passion for problem-solving.
